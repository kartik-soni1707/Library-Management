Important pointers:

Duration:

We request you to return the book in a reasonable amount of time since other members may also be in the waiting list. Books are requested to be returned within 7 days.

In case of delay of a request:

In case a specific book you have ordered is not available due to circulation we will ensure that we send you an alternate book from this request form.

In case of delay in return of an issued book :

A fine of rupees 1 (per day) is charged after 1 week of issue. In case of any confusion please approach the library in-charge.

In case of losing a book:

If a book that you have been issued gets lost, please ensure that the price of the lost book is paid before futher issuing.
